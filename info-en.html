How to get there

Getting to us is easy both by car or train. If you travel by car, take the exit from road B3 to Friedberg and follow the signs to the Central Railway Station. You can use the parking lot there (costs €1,20 per hour). If you travel by train, take the line S6 from Frankfurt to Friedberg (Hessen). Our store is about 3 minutes walk from the railway station.

About us

We started our grocery store in December 2022. It had been our dream for a long while to have a store where various Hungarian food is available and our childhood memories can be brought back with those well known tastes and smells. We wanted to provide those living far away from the mother country with a piece of home.

Customers from any other countries can satisfy their taste and will for sure find something to make them happy.
Come by, have a chat with us and feel yourself home.
